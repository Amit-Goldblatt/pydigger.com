Explanations

On the PyDigger site we collect meta-data about packages uploaded to PyPI. During the data collection we encounter issues with some of the meta-data. In these articles we discuss the fields, how to we collect the information, what might be issues with our data collection, and how certain issues related to those fields can be fixed.
